BAZI 八字 Maps you to the earth

BaZi (八字, "eight characters") is a 3,000-year-old Chinese system that maps your birth date onto the Five Elements — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water.

It doesn't look to distant planets. It looks to the season you were born in, the energy of your year, the element of your day.

Not what star were you born under — but what are you made of.
